About the Team

  • Winner’s Circle Robo Jockeys was started by DuPont Manual High School in 2014.
  • WCRJ is open to high school students from the community and includes roughly 40 student members from eight high schools, eight coaches, and fifteen mentors.
  • In our rookie year we ranked 10th place in the Saint Louis Regional and 12th place in the Smoky Mountains Regional Roughly half our team are girls, half are from DuPont Manual High School and the remaining half come from seven local high schools and several home schools.
  • Notable Sponsors include GE FirstBuild, University of Louisville Speed School, Big Ass Solutions, Dow, NASA, NSF, Ford, UAW and Papa Johns Pizza
  • We won two Rookie Inspiration Awards and a Highest Rookie Seed Award during our rookie year.
  • In our community we have received the Spirit of Louisville Appreciation Award and the Pegasus Parade Honorable Float Mention.
  • Semi-finalist at Smokey Mountain Regional Tournament

Winner’s Circle Robo Jockeys is an award winning second-year First Robotics Competition Team from Louisville, Kentucky
