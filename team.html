Students

Our students come from eight local high schools, including DuPont Manual, Gatton Academy, Atherton, Eastern, Fairdale, Christian Academy, Ballard, South Oldham, and several home school groups.

Coaches

Our coaches include parents and mentors with engineering, business, and design expertise working at various organizations, including University of Louisville, UPS, GE, FirstBuild, and others.

Mentors

WCRJ’s mentors come from many local organizations, including University of Louisville Speed School (faculty and students), GE and FirstBuild, FrankDesign, Dow, UPS, duPont Manual High School, Big Ass Solutions, SCORE, and KY Innovation Network. We are always eager to recruit new mentors to help guide our team members in learning valuable engineering and business skills through hands-on participation on the team.
